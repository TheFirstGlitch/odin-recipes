Homemade Mac & Cheese

Description

A recipe that is sure to coat every nook and cranny of your noodles with creamy smooth cheese. This cheese delight is topped with buttered bread crumbs to give it that nice little crispy crunch.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
